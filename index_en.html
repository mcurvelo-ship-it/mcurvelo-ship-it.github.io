Research Focus

Mechanics & Interfaces

Clip, magnet and screw mounts; swappable without interfering with sensors or cooling. Focus: safety, tolerances, service time.

Materials & Coatings

TPU/PC/PA compounds, ESD options, anti-soil topcoats. Lab tests for adhesion, abrasion, UV, temperature/humidity, cleaning agents.

CE Pathway

Conformity matrix, risk analysis, labeling. Clear pathway for aftermarket kits—without altering the base machine.

Materials Path & Validation

Lab Partnerships

Collaboration with an accredited institute for rigorous test reports as a basis for grants and pilots.

Design for Service

Replaceable modules, low-residue removal, fleet ID/QR options for asset management.

Sustainability

Modularity reduces waste: replace damaged sub-modules instead of entire panels.
