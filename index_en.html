Research Focus

Mechanics & Interfaces

Clip, magnetic and screw mounts — replaceable without affecting sensors or cooling. Focus: safety, tolerances, assembly time.

Materials & Coatings

TPU/PC/PA compounds, ESD options, anti-soil coatings. Tests for adhesion, abrasion, UV, temperature/humidity and cleaning agents.

CE Compliance

Compliance matrix, risk analysis, labeling. Aftermarket kits that do not modify the base unit.

Material Path & Validation

Lab Partnerships

Collaboration with an accredited institute for robust test reports as a basis for grants and pilots.

Design for Service

Replaceable modules, low-residue disassembly, ID labeling for fleet management, QR/ID options.

Sustainability

Modularity reduces waste — damaged areas are replaced as submodules instead of full panels.
