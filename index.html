EU design • CI-conform roboskins

The next evolution of robotics begins at the surface.

Cybershell Technologies develops adaptive outer modules (“roboskins”) for humanoid robots, uniting design, function and brand identity.

Manufactured from high-performance TPU/PC/PA composites with optional carbon or Kevlar layers, the modules offer high impact strength, UV and chemical resistance, and verified fire behavior per EU standards — essential for CE conformity and series approval.

Each surface is executed in the operator’s corporate design: colors, logos and textures become functional elements of the outer shell.

The modular architecture enables zone-wise replacement, planned maintenance and sustainable material cycles within European manufacturing.

Developed with accredited test laboratories and produced in the EU — for B2B pilot partners and pre-order programs from 2026.

Aftermarket-compatible without touching the base system: documented mounting paths, CI-conform surfaces and a clear CE test route — ready for robust, brand-visible field deployment.

Features

Engineering-grade: Precision & Fit

Engineering-grade precision: tight tolerances, clearly defined mounting zones, and reproducible fit across all series.

Material technology

High-performance TPU/PC/PA composites; optional carbon/Kevlar layers for impact and abrasion protection.

Surface options

High-gloss (piano black / high-gloss finish), satin/matte, carbon finish, micro-textures, partial grip zones; printed or CI color-matched.

Serviceability

Screw and clip concepts, quick-swap for high-wear areas.

Traceability

Serial identification, spare-parts mapping, documentation.

Circularity & sustainability

Mono-material disassembly, take-back & re-granulate options; material tracking per zone.

Compliance & production

EU development and manufacturing. Testing according to relevant EU standards in cooperation with accredited labs (CE pathway in preparation). Material, fire and aging tests are implemented step by step. Supply chain and production are designed for B2B series.

Note: Cybershell is an independent aftermarket provider. Compatibility and mounting are carried out within legal frameworks; the robot manufacturer’s warranty is not affected as long as no unauthorised interventions are made on the base system. (Details in the datasheet/FAQ.)

FAQ (excerpt)

How is the manufacturer’s warranty considered?

Our modules are exterior add-on parts. Mounting is intended without intervention in safety-relevant systems. Always check your manufacturer’s warranty terms; we provide documentation and mounting guidance.

Which certifications are planned?

Material, fire and environmental tests as part of the CE pathway. Results and standard references are provided to pilot customers.

Minimum quantities?

From batch size 50+ units per variant in series; small pilot batches on request.
