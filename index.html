Research Focus

Mechanics & Interfaces

Clip, magnet and screw mounts; swap without touching sensors/cooling. Focus: safety, tolerances, assembly time.

Materials & Coatings

TPU/PC/PA composites, ESD options, anti-soil topcoats. Lab tests: adhesion, abrasion, UV, temperature/humidity, cleaning agents.

CE Compliance

Compliance matrix, risk analysis, labeling. Aftermarket kit pathway — no changes to the base unit.

Material Path & Validation

Laboratory Partnerships

Collaboration with an accredited institute for robust test reports supporting grants & pilot deployments.

Design for Service

Replaceable modules, low-residue removal, labeling for fleet management, optional QR/ID features.

Sustainability

Modularity reduces waste — damaged zones are replaced as sub-modules instead of entire panels.
